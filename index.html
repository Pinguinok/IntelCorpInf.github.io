Intel Corporation

Manufacturer of electronic devices and computer components

Guide. Chronology of leaders:

  • Robert Neuss - President and Chief Executive Officer, 1968-1975
  • Gordon Moore - President of the company in 1975-1979, chief executive officer in 1975-1987
  • Andy Grove - president of the company in 1979-1997, chief executive officer in 1987-1998
  • Craig Barrett - President of the company in 1997-2002, CEO in 1998-2005
  • Paul Otellini - President of the company in 2002-2013, Chief Executive Officer in 2005-2013
  • Rene James - President of the company in 2013-2015
  • Brian Krzhanich - Chief Executive Officer in 2013—2018

Current Guide:

  • Andrew Bryant - Chairman of the Board of Directors since 2012, in the company since 1981. Also on the boards of the Columbia Sportswear Company and McKesson Corporation
  • Robert Swan - Chief Executive Officer since January 30, 2019; From 2016 to 2018, he was Chief Financial Officer. Prior to that, he was chief financial officer of eBay Inc. (where continues to be a member of the board of directors). He began his career in the finance department of General Electric (from 1985 to 2000)
